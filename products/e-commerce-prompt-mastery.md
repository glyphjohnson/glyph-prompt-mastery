## Introduction {#introduction}

### What Are AI Prompts?

AI prompts are the instructions you give to large language models (LLMs) like Grok, GPT, or Claude to produce desired outputs. Effective prompts turn vague ideas into high-quality, targeted content.

**Core Elements of a Good Prompt:**
- **Role**: Define who the AI is (e.g., 'You are an expert e-commerce copywriter').
- **Context**: Provide background info.
- **Task**: Clearly state what to do.
- **Format**: Specify structure (bullets, length, tone).
- **Examples**: Few-shot learning for style.
- **Constraints**: Keywords, word count, SEO rules.

**Zero-Shot vs. Few-Shot:**
- Zero-shot: Direct task.
- Few-shot: Include 1-3 examples.

### Why Prompts for E-Commerce?

E-commerce thrives on compelling content:
- **Product Descriptions**: Drive conversions by 20-30% with persuasive copy.
- **SEO Titles**: Improve click-through rates (CTR) by ranking higher.
- **Emails**: Boost open rates (25%+) and recovery sales (10-15%).

AI prompts enable:
- Scalability for thousands of products.
- Consistency across brands.
- Personalization at scale.
- A/B testing variants.

### Prompt Engineering Basics {#prompt-basics}

1. **Specificity**: &quot;The more details, the better.&quot;
2. **Tone & Voice**: Match brand (fun, professional, luxury).
3. **Call to Action (CTA)**: Always include.
4. **SEO Integration**: Natural keywords.
5. **Iteration**: Refine based on outputs.

**Example Evolution:**
```
V1: &quot;Describe a phone.&quot;
V2: &quot;Write a 150-word description for iPhone 15, highlight camera, battery, design. Bullet features, exciting tone.&quot;
```

## Product Descriptions {#product-descriptions}

### Best Practices {#best-practices-descriptions}

- Length: 100-300 words.
- Structure: Hook, features/benefits, specs, CTA.
- Benefits over features.
- Sensory language.
- Bullet points for scannability.
- Keywords naturally.

### Prompt Templates {#prompt-templates-descriptions}

**Basic Template:**
```
You are [role]. Write a [length]-word product description for [product]. Features: [list]. [Tone]. Structure: Hook, bullets, CTA. Include keywords: [keywords].
```

**Advanced:**
```
Act as [role]. Step 1: List benefits. Step 2: Write engaging hook. Step 3: Format with bullets. Product: [product]. Constraints: [rules].
```

### Examples {#examples-descriptions}

#### 1. Fashion - Organic Cotton T-Shirt (Example 1/40+)

**Prompt:**
```
You are a sustainable fashion copywriter. Write a 200-word description for men's organic cotton t-shirt. Features: 100% GOTS-certified cotton, breathable, 5 colors, S-XXL, pre-shrunk. Emphasize eco-friendliness, comfort, versatility. Tone: casual eco-conscious. Structure: Hook paragraph, bullet features, CTA.
```

**AI Output:**
```
Elevate your everyday wardrobe with our Men's Organic Cotton T-Shirt – where comfort meets conscience.

Crafted from 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton, this tee is softer than a cloud and breathable for all-day wear. Pre-shrunk to maintain its perfect fit wash after wash, it's available in 5 timeless colors and sizes S-XXL.

**Key Features:**
- 100% organic cotton – kind to skin and planet
- Breathable fabric for ultimate comfort
- Pre-shrunk, no surprises in the laundry
- Versatile style for casual or layered looks

Go green without sacrificing style. **Grab yours today and wear the change!**
```

**Tips:** Role sets expertise, structure ensures readability.

## SEO Titles {#seo-titles}

### SEO Best Practices {#best-practices-seo}

- 50-60 characters.
- Keyword front-loaded.
- Brand at end.
- Numbers, power words (Best, Ultimate).
- Question or how-to if fits.

### Prompt Templates {#prompt-templates-seo}

**Template:**
```
Generate 10 SEO titles for [product], max 60 chars. Front-load [main keyword]. Compelling, include [power words]. List numbered.
```

### Examples {#examples-seo}

## Marketing Emails {#marketing-emails}

### Best Practices {#best-practices-emails}

- Subject: 40-50 chars, curiosity.
- Preheader: Teaser.
- Personalization: {name}.
- Urgency/scarcity.
- Images placeholders.
- CTA buttons.

### Prompt Templates {#prompt-templates-emails}

**Template:**
```
Write [type] email for [store]. Subject, preheader, body. Personalize {name}. Product: [product]. Offer: [discount]. HTML structure, short paragraphs.
```

### Examples {#examples-emails}

#### 1. Abandoned Cart Recovery (Example 1)

**Prompt:**
```
You are an e-commerce email marketer. Write abandoned cart email. Customer {name} left 'iPhone case' in cart. Subject, preheader, body with urgency, 10% off code SAVE10. CTA buy now. Tone: friendly urgent.
```

**AI Output:**
**Subject:** {name}, don't forget your iPhone case! 10% off  

**Preheader:** Complete your purchase before it's gone...

**Body:**
Hi {name},

We noticed you left a sleek iPhone case in your cart. Protect your phone in style!

[Image placeholder]

**Get 10% off with code SAVE10** – offer ends soon!

[Buy Now button]

Happy shopping,  
[Store] Team

**Tips:** Personalization + urgency boosts recovery.

#### 2. Welcome Email (Example 2)

Write welcome email for new subscriber {name}. Offer 15% first purchase. Highlight bestsellers. Subject catchy, body engaging, CTA shop now.

## Advanced Techniques {#advanced-techniques}

- **Chain of Thought**: 'Think step by step before writing.'
- **Few-Shot**: Provide 2-3 examples.
- **Temperature**: Low for consistency, high for creativity.
- **A/B Variants**: Generate 3 versions.
- **Personalization**: Insert variables.
- **Multilingual**: Specify language.
- **Integration**: Use with tools like Zapier.
