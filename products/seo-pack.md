## SEO Titles & Meta Prompts (20+) {#prompts}

### Core Titles (10)
```
1. Optimal Length: \"Generate 10 SEO-optimized titles for [PRODUCT], under 60 characters, front-load [MAIN KW], include [SECONDARY KWS].\"

2. Persuasive Tone: \"Create 8 compelling SEO titles for [PRODUCT] in [TONE: e.g. urgent, luxury] style, optimized for [KW].\"

3. Feature-Focused: \"10 titles highlighting [KEY FEATURE] for [PRODUCT], SEO KW: [LIST].\"

4. Benefit-Driven: \"SEO titles emphasizing [BENEFITS] for [PRODUCT]. Generate 10, <60 chars.\"

5. Urgency: \"10 urgency-driven SEO titles for [PRODUCT SALE], KW [KW].\"

6. Question Style: \"Question-based SEO titles for [PRODUCT], 10 variants, KW front-loaded.\"

7. Numbered Lists: \"Numbered SEO titles like 'Top 5 [KW] for [PRODUCT]' , 10 ideas.\"

8. Social Proof: \"Titles with 'Best-Selling [PRODUCT]' or reviews, 10 SEO optimized.\"

9. Seasonal: \"Holiday-themed SEO titles for [PRODUCT], KW [KW].\"

10. Comparison: \"'Vs Competitor' style SEO titles for [PRODUCT], 10.\"
```
